Craig Large Carrying Case - Orange The Craig Carrying Case from Delfonics is a roomy pouch that can be used to hold a 13" laptop, a portfolio of documents, and other work essentials. Slim enough to fit into a messenger bag or backpack, the Craig Carrying Case features a gold-toned zipper closure. The case can also be used as an oversized clutch that will easily hold a phone, large wallet, and other items. In addition to the zippered compartment, the case includes a front pouch that is secured by two gold-toned snaps when the case is folded over. The smartly designed case also delivers a secret message within the front compartment: "It all depends on how you see and interpret things."
