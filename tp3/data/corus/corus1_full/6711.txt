Toshiba Digitizer Pen for WT310 008 PA5133U-1EUC This Toshiba Digitizer Pen is designed to work with Toshiba pen-enabled devices. It lets you interact with your device in a more natural way by incorporating the convenient and intuitive aspects of pen and paper into the PC/tablet experience. Using this digitizer pen, you can write directly on the screen and save your notes in your own handwriting - or convert them to typed text for use in other applications. The pen can also handle common mouse and keyboard tasks like opening applications, selecting text and displaying menus. Since the grid provides the power to the pen through resonant coupling, no batteries are required. Keep a spare pen handy.
