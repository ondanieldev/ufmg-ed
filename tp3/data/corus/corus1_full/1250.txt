Antec 30125 VERIS Elite Multimedia Station The Multimedia Station Elite model installs into one external 5.25 drive bay and features a stylish, programmable VFD display and convenient volume control knob that are both accessible right from the front of your PC case! The Elites VFD screen is easily configurable to display all kinds of information, from your system specs to your current available hard drive space to your email status and much more! The Elite Multimedia Station is powered by an internal USB connector and features a convenient volume control knob right on the display. The Elite also comes with the RM200 remote control, which is a larger, more functional remote for quick and easy handling of your media files. This remote control can even be used as a mouse!
