Test Big Data 9658 The Viking IntelliFlash USB flash memory reader makes it easy for you to  transfer files between your digital device and PC or Mac. An all-in-one solution  for high-speed bidirectional image and data transfer, this reader downloads  images and data quickly from flash memory cards used in many applications,  including digital cameras, PDAs, laptops, and MP3 players. The USB connection  delivers data transfer rates of 1.5 MBps and is hot swappable, letting you  insert and remove the reader between host devices without turning the power off. The reader supports the most popular flash card formats, including CompactFlash,  SmartMedia, and PC Cards.
