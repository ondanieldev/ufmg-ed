Protronix 2-Port PCI-Express SATA III 6Gbps RAID Controller Card with Cable, Low Profile Ready Add 2 internal SATA III (6Gbps) ports to a computer using a PCI Express x1 slot. SATA III ensures maximum transfer speed for high speed hard drives and solid state drives (SSDs). The optional RAID feature allows you to mirror the drives for added protection or to strip them for increased capacity and performance. A low profile bracket is included so that the card can also be used in small form factor computers. A SATA cable is included.
