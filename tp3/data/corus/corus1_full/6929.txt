barnett Football Back Plate The Barnett removable football back plate provides lower back protection to football players who want to protect their vulnerable blind sides from dangerous hits. This back plate has grommets that allow the player to raise or lower the back plate to a comfortable position. It attaches to any football shoulder pads. Football back plates are ideal for running backs and any other football players that want to protect their spines, kidneys, and lower back from punishing hits.
