Toshiba USB 3.0 Gigabit Ethernet Adapter The Toshiba USB 3.0 to Gigabit Ethernet Adapter lets you add one RJ-45 Ethernet port to your USB-enabled laptop or desktop computer quickly and easily. Simply plug into a USB 3.0 port and enjoy data transfer rates of up to 1000 Mbps over your Gigabit network. This compact adapter is USB 3.0 compliance and backward compatible with USB 2.0 or earlier version. It is an ideal network solution for both home and business environment and a great alternative for a gaming console that required network access but without Wi-Fi. It is USB bus-powered and does not require power adapter.
