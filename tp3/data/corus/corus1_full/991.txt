Belkin Cable Dome Cable Organizer Cream (Pack of 3) The Cable Dome organizes cables in any area in your home or office into an elegant, inexpensive solution. The Dome mounts easily with the enclosed screws or heavy-duty double-sided tape -- to the underside of desks or counters, on walls, and behind your entertainment center.  Simply connect a few of the Domes to the surface and route the cords through either the large or small cable channels.  You can add, remove, and reroute cables as needed.  The Dome keeps them safe, hidden from view, and easily accessible.    Packaging Includes: * 3 Cable Domes  * 3 1/2" screws * 3 pieces of heavy-duty double-sided tape
