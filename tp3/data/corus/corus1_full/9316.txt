8 Channel USB GPIO Module With Analog Inputs Numatos 8 Channel USB GPIO module is low cost easily deployable solution for connecting your PC to other electronic circuitry through USB. No USB specific knowledge is required to set up and use this module. Features:: 8 TTL (3.3V) compatible GPIOs available, 6 Analog inputs All GPIOs can be individually configured as input or output, Can be controlled by using standard serial console applications or custom applications, No USB protocol knowledge required, On board controller can be reprogrammed for custom applications.
