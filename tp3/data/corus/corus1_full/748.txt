CODI KEY CABLE LOCK The security and portability of our CODi Key Cable Lock allows traveling professionals to secure equipment on the road and at the office. The unique design features convenient push-button technology for keyless locking in universal kensingon type security slots of laptops and desktop computers. A hardened steel lock head and 6' coated, galvanized steel cable ensure superior strength and theft protection. Included with the lock is a stainless steel washer for securing desktop chassis closures.
