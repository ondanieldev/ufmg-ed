ScubaPro Mantis 2.0 M2 USB Cradle After a day in the water it's fun to extend the diving experience for a few more hours by reliving and analyzing your day's dives. This is easily done with a download cradle specially designed for your particular SCUBAPRO dive computer. With a cradle you can download your dives to a PC or MAC laptop or desktop computer using a USB connection and the LogTRAK software that comes with your dive computer. The process is easy and it's lots of fun. It lets you relive your dives, it provides visual aids when sharing your dives stories with friends and family, plus by studying the details of your dives you can increase your diving skills.
