Blueprint HID Hub 8 Site, HUB-8 The Blueprint HID Hub 8 Site, HUB-8, allows gardeners to efficiently and automatically control lighting in indoor gardens. The HID Hub controls up to eight 1000W HID ballasts, including both MH and HPS lighting systems. An integrated trigger cable facilitates any timed lighting sequence when connected to a standard 24-hour timing device. Features eight universal outlets for a maximum lighting wattage of 8000W. The HID Hub is wall-mountable and comes with a three-year warranty.
