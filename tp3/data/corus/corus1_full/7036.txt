Surface Music Kit- Microsoft Remix Project The Surface Music Kit lets you remix your favorite music and share it with the world. You can also start from scratch and create a song from the backbeat up. The Surface Music Kit is designed for Surface 2, Surface Pro 2, and Surface Pro running Windows 8.1. For Surface Pro, update to Windows 8.1 to get started with the Surface Music Kit. See, Update Surface from Windows 8 to Windows 8.1 for info on how to update. The Surface Music Kit isn't compatible with Surface RT.
