Classic TV Pong Game MiniKit - MK121NTSC by Velleman. A beginner level soldering kit. Pong was the very first sports arcade video game back in the 70s. Now you can build it and play it in your game room at home. The MK121NTSC from Velleman has both a one and two player mode. With four difficulty levels you can go from beginner to expert in no time. It's important to get and keep the serve as on as only the server can score points. You even have the ability to deliver a "smash" shot for that knock blow!
