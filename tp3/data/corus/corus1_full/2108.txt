Intellinet Networks Adsl Modem Splitter/Adapter (201124) Minimize ADSL noise on your phone line with the Intellinet Network Solutions ADSL Modem Splitter/Adapter. With one RJ11 connector at one end for the telephone line and two RJ11 connectors at the other end for fax and your ADSL modem, the ADSL Modem Splitter/Adapter protects the voice signal and ADSL digital signal from the interference of a telephone and fax machine.
