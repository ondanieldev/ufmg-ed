BT-iSpeaker Bluetooth Adapter For Speaker The BT- iSpeaker is a Bluetooth Adapter for speakers of any kind.  Simply plug the adapter in your wall outlet, connect your speaker, system, home theater system, etc. with the Audio cable included then pair your Bluetooth enabled device and now you can play your music wirelessly.  For highest quality audio, use your bluetooth device within 30-45 feet of the adapter.    Supported Bluetooth types: A2DP, AVRCP, V2.0, CLASS 2
