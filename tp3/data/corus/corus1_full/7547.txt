Montolit Masterpiuma Mosakit Pad The Raimondi Mosakit Pad is a specially designed mat that upgrades your manual tile cutter into one that cuts mosaic tiles too. The Mosakit accomodates mosaic tiles up to 14" and is the perfect accessory for cutting your most fragile tiles, as well as cutting glass mosaics. Use in combination with Masterpiuma tile cutter for cutting mosaic tiles. Easily attaches to the tile cutter. For use on up to 14" mosaic tile. Works great on glass tile mosaics. Fits the following snap cutters: Raimondi Push/Pull Cutters, Montolit Push/Pull Cutters, QEP Porcelain King Push Cutters
