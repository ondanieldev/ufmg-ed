NEW! Case for BeagleBone Black - Jet Black assemble in 30 seconds by SB Components This is an elegant and innovative protective case designed specifically for the Beagle Bone Black.  The case is a two-piece injection-moulded ABS enclosure that snaps together around the Beagle Bone. It provides tough protection for the Beagle Bone Black while providing access to all of the board's connectors. The case has light pipes for the boards LEDs and includes a reset key so the BeagleBone Black can be reset without having to open the case.
