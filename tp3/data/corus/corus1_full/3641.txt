Microsoft Wireless Mobile Mouse 3500 Limited Edition Studio Series - Linn Olofsdotter This Microsoft 3500 Limited Edition Studio Series wireless mouse features a design by artist Linn Olofsdotter. Thanks to its BlueTrack technology, this mouse can be used on almost any surface and has exceptional tracking accuracy. The tiny nano transceiver plugs into your USB port and can be stored inside the mouse when not in use. This mouse can be used for up to eight months without a battery change.
