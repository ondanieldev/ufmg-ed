SIIG 4.2A USB Power Adapter 2-Port Black (AC-PW0J12-S1) SIIGs 4.2A USB Power Adapter - 2-Port (Black) is a compact USB wall charger quickly and easily charges up to two USB powered devices at one time. It provides 2 USB ports to support charging up to 2 tablet computers, smartphones and other USB powered devices simultaneously, and features folding power plug for greater portability.
