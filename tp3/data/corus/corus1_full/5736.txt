Sony Portable Wireless Server (WGC10/N) Portable Wireless Server is your answer for sharing photos and videos while on the go. Connect your smartphone to the Portable Wireless Server via Wi-Fi and backup data, free up space or share content. You can also use the Portable Wireless Server to provide power to your smartphone. Portable Wireless Server is compatible with both Android and iOS based smartphones and tablets. After purchasing the PWS, simply install the free PWS Manager app from Google Play or the App Store. Once the app is installed on your smartphone or tablet, you are ready to connect to the Portable Wireless Server to transfer or backup content.
