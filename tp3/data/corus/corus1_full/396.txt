Pioneer PDA - 5002 Video Card The Pioneer PDA-5002 video board adds video and digital RGB ( DVI ) inputs to the Pioneer PDP-503CMX Flex Plasma Display. Installing this card makes the display compatible with High Definition video and digital RGB input. Advantages of digital RGB input include clear, noise-free image and data presentation. With DVI you can connect the computer to the display, and the appropriate screen size is set automatically.
