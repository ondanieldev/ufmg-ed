FITTIO MOH-FTRBK Black Wrist Rest Fatigue reduction wrist rest "FITTIO (Fitio)" born by joint development with the Yokohama municipal rehabilitation center. It realizes a high operability that was derived by myoelectric potential measurement and test evaluation of body pressure distribution. By designing to a shape which gently fits in the hand, it is demonstrating the maximum pressure dispersion performance to the maximum. We use domestically produced ultra flexible material "EXGEL (Exel)" which has a track record in fields such as medical care, welfare and race car supplies. It reduces the burden on the wrist with excellent body pressure dispersion performance. The surface material is using high quality synthetic leather that is also used in medical practice. Even if it gets dirty it can be wiped off with water, detergent, alcohol, etc. and it can be kept clean. Specifications Size: Width 80 x depth 60 x height 18 mm Material: [Surface] Polyurethane synthetic leather, [Back side] Polyurethane, [Internal] Synthetic rubber body weight: 56 g. ELECOM Co., Ltd. is a Japanese electronics company, headquartered in Osaka, Japan. They are best known for their modern design, especially with their mice. The company produces many electronics along with other Japanese computer-peripheral manufacturers such as I-O Data, Buffalo, and Century micro that are shipped to many outside and larger companies, as an OEM (e.g. Century Micro is known to ship their memory chips to Samsung.) In recent years, its business has been expanded into new areas, including: supplies of tablet PCs and smartphones, health care products such as body composition scales and electric therapy equipment, and the digital home area, with an enhanced development of network products such as wireless LAN routers, a cloud service and an installation service for wireless LAN access points.
