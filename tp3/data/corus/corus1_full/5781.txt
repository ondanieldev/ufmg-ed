Delfonics Craig Carrying Case - Dark Blue The Craig Carrying Case from Delfonics is a roomy pouch that can be used to hold an iPad mini, pens and pencils, notecards, and other work essentials. Petite enough to fit into a messenger bag or purse, the Craig Carrying Case features a gold-toned zipper closure. The case can also be used as a clutch that will easily hold a phone, wallet, and keys. In addition to the zippered compartment, the case includes a front pouch that is secured by two gold-toned snaps when the case is folded over. The smartly designed case also delivers a secret message within the front compartment: "It all depends on how you see and interpret things."
