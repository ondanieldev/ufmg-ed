Apricorn mSATA Wire Enclosure and Upgrade Kit (AMSW-USB3) mSATA Wire is the easiest way to upgrade mSATA based notebooks or desktop systems to a faster, higher capacity mSATA module. Upgrade in 3 easy steps - connect hardware, run EZ Gig cloning software, and swap out the modules. Then, use the old mSATA module in the mSATA Wire enclosure to create a super fast USB 3.0 external SSD drive that can reach speeds in excess of 300MBs! mSATA Wire includes mSATA module enclosure and EZ Gig cloning software (Windows) and Mac cloning instructions.
