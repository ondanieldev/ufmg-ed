Developer NFC Tag Kit - 35 pack - Mifare Ultralight EV1, Mifare Standard 1k, ICODE SLIX, NTAG210 Micro, NTAG213, NTAG215, NTAG216 A consolidated package containing some of the most commonly used NFC chip types. Multiple form factors and sizes to help you select the best NFC chip type for your projects. Information about NFC chip types, NFC encoding hardware and developer resources are available from GoToTags. WARNING: NFC tags cannot be used on metal, aluminum or electronic surfaces. A designated on-metal NFC tag should be used if usage on those surfaces is required to prevent interference.
