United States Air Force Vietnam The Air Force played a crucial role in the struggle in Vietnam and throughout Southeast Asia beginning in 1964. This dramatic color film depicts the build-up of personnel and equipment, plus all the latest (at the time) USAF aircraft, including the Super Sabre's, F-105's, F-111's, B-52's, F-104's, Sidewinder missiles and many more. ALSO ON THE SPECIAL DVD Edition: 'The Battle Of Khe Sanh' Considered to be the best documentary of the Vietnam conflict, and it contains some of the most intense air attacks in photographic history. 'Screaming Eagles In Vietnam' This excellent documentary demonstrates the bravery and skill of the men of the 101st Airborne division - the famous Screaming Eagles. All Color Running Time 81 Minutes, DVD
