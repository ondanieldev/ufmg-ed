MuxLab 500039-2PK Stereo Hi-Fi Video Balun Male, 2-Pack The Stereo Hi-Fi/Video Balun allows a single composite video signal to be transmitted via unshielded twisted pair (UTP) cable up to 2200' (670m) in a point-to-point connection. The Stereo Hi-Fi/Video Balun features full audio bandwidth response for high fidelity applications and features built-in color-coded cable leads for ease of installation. The Stereo Hi-Fi/Video Balun also works in conjunction with MuXLab' s Audio-Video Distribution Hub (p/n 500200) to allow composite video programming to be distributed via UTP. Some of the applications are; classroom video distribution, commercial and home audio/video systems, hospital video training, video conferencing and video kiosks.
